## 4. Testable Predictions
- If the preferred `PISA` story is real, a leave-one-unit-out or anchor-only conditioning score with proper survey variance will still show heavy compression of raw gaps, `uncertainty_data` near zero, and `space_shape` negative in most leave-one-country-out runs.
- If the residual `PISA` story is mostly artifact, the family order will reorder or collapse once target-item contamination, framework recoding, and country heterogeneity are handled.
- If the school-pipeline explanation is real, deep `NLSY97` transcript payloads will explain `Math Knowledge` and transcript GPA together more than they explain `PIAT` or `Arithmetic Reasoning`; the female residual should stay concentrated on learned-course surfaces within narrow exposure sets.
- If the early-school direction really is settled, composition-aware reweighting or a non-reading bridge will shrink the `NLSCYA`/`ECLS` magnitude gap without reopening the sign conflict.
- If process/interface effects are still underestimated, matched response-time/omission/revisit analyses in `PISA` will attenuate residual `space_shape` more than transcript-style schooling explanations would predict.
