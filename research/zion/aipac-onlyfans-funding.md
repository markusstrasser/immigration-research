# AIPAC Funding: Leonid Radvinsky / OnlyFans Connection

## Claim

"OnlyFans is the main funder of AIPAC." — Shaiel Ben-Ephraim (@academic_la), March 2, 2026
[SOURCE: https://x.com/academic_la/status/2028560974278762726]

## Verified Facts

### Radvinsky's $11M Pledge

Leonid Radvinsky, majority owner (~75%) of OnlyFans, and his wife Katie Chudnovsky pledged **$11 million** to AIPAC after October 7, 2023. This was the **largest single individual pledge** in leaked internal AIPAC donor documents obtained by *The Lever*.

- The donation was listed under "Mr. Anonymous Anonymous" on AIPAC's internal donor list
- *The Lever* identified Radvinsky through personal details matching the listing and a documented wire transfer from Chudnovsky
- Radvinsky denied the donation: "I didn't donate or pledge $11M" — applicable to himself, his foundation, and his family
- He declined to explain why he appeared on the donor list or address the wire transfer evidence

[SOURCE: The Lever, "Inside the Israel Lobby's $90 Million War Chest," Feb 1, 2024 — https://www.levernews.com/inside-the-israel-lobbys-new-90-million-war-chest/]
[SOURCE: Rolling Stone, "OnlyFans Owner Pledged $11 Million to Israel Lobby: Report," Feb 1, 2024 — https://www.rollingstone.com/politics/politics-news/onlyfans-owner-leonid-radvinsky-11-million-aipac-1234958976/]

### AIPAC's Post-Oct. 7 Fundraising

- AIPAC raised ~$90 million in Oct–Dec 2023 following the Oct. 7 attack
- Prior monthly average in 2023 was ~$12 million/month
- The $11M Radvinsky pledge was ~12% of the $90M post-Oct. 7 haul
- The next largest pledges were significantly smaller ($2–3M range, e.g., Jonathan Gray / Blackstone connections)

[SOURCE: The Lever, Feb 2024]

## Assessment

| Claim Element | Verdict |
|---|---|
| Radvinsky (OnlyFans owner) was AIPAC's largest single donor post-Oct. 7 | **Supported** by leaked internal documents; subject denies |
| "OnlyFans" funds AIPAC | **Misleading** — personal donation from Radvinsky, not corporate |
| "Main funder" of AIPAC | **Exaggerated** — largest single pledge ≠ main funder of a $100M+ operation |

**More accurate framing:** Leonid Radvinsky, whose wealth derives from OnlyFans, was reportedly AIPAC's largest individual donor in its post-Oct. 7 fundraising push, with an $11M pledge constituting ~12% of the $90M raised in that period.

**Evidence level:** Contested — based on leaked documents from one outlet (*The Lever*); subject denies; no independent corroboration of the donor list itself, though Rolling Stone, The Forward, and Jacobin reported on The Lever's findings.
